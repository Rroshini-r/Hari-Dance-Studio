Hari Dance Studio

Hari Dance Studio delivers dance training to Auckland club members at their Parnell studio. They primarily focus on nurturing young dancers into industry professionals and will continue to see this vision in the future. They offer a variety of classes in various genres and styles to dancers of all ages and abilities. Conveniently located just a short walk from the Ferry Building in the Viaduct. We have access to three dance studios.
